Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This extension does not use Google Analytics or third-party tracking tools. We do not collect analytics information such as your IP address, browser type, device type, or time spent in the extension for tracking purposes.

We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it (for example, through direct communication).

What is sent for analysis
  • Extracted HTML (may be truncated)
  • Extracted external CSS (may be truncated)
  • Used only to generate suggestions

Tip: avoid analyzing pages with sensitive content (e.g., banking, private dashboards). Use the toggles to redact form values and remove scripts before extraction.
